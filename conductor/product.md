# Initial Concept
Outils utiles pour les Forgerons, fait par Zaaees (A suite of useful tools for Minecraft blacksmiths).

# Product Guide

## Vision
"Le Forgeron sans Enclume" is a comprehensive web-based utility designed to streamline the operations of Minecraft blacksmiths and enchanters. It provides essential calculators for item pricing, repair/fusion XP costs, smelting optimization, and enchanted gear valuation, all while managing transactions through an integrated note system.

## Target Audience
- Minecraft players specializing as Blacksmiths (Forgerons) or Enchanters (Enchanteurs) within a multiplayer economy (e.g., Agoralis).
- Server administrators managing the economy and player roles.

## Key Features
- **Pricing Calculator:** Optimize buying and selling of items, including profit calculations.
- **Repair & Fusion:** Compute XP and anvil wear costs for repairing or fusing gear.
- **Smelting Optimization:** Determine the ideal number of blast furnaces needed to reach revenue goals.
- **Enchantment Valuation:** Calculate the total price of enchanted gear based on base items and selected enchantments.
- **Catalog Management:** Maintain a dynamic catalog of materials, armor, tools, and books.
- **Transaction Notes:** A built-in notebook to track ongoing, pending, or urgent transactions.
- **Authentication & Roles:** Secure access with role-based permissions (Blacksmith, Enchanter, Visitor, Admin).
